The General Administrative Structure Of MSAU

The Delegates' Conference (DC)

The Delegates' Conference as per the MSAU constitution is the supreme body and sits every three years. It has the powers to amend the constitution and to elect a new G.C.

The Governing Council (GC)

The G.C sits at least once a year to among other things elect a new NEC which like the REC and DEC has a term of only one year in office. The National Patron is the chairman of the G.C. The NEC is the most executive body that runs the affairs of the association. It is headed by the President. The National Patron is advisor to the NEC.

The Board of Trustees (BOT)

The Board of Trustees composed of five members who will elect the chairman among themselves and the patron of MSAU shall be the secretary to this Board.
For the purpose of the legal ownership of the property of MSAU the Board of Trustees have powers to buy, manage, dispose off or modify MSAU property as per development needs of the association.

The National Executive Council (NEC)

The NEC is the most executive body that runs the affairs of the association and is composed of eighteen members. It is headed by the President.

The Regional Executive Council (REC)

The REC is made up of all District Council Chairmen together with an elected executive. The Regional Councilor of each region to the NEC is the Chairman of the REC. The REC has a Sub- Constitution derived out of this NEC constitution and it details regional level activities. It appoints its Regional Patron.

The District Executive Council (DEC)

The DEC is composed of all MSA chairmen together with an elected executive. The DEC has a Sub- Constitution derived out of the REC constitution and it details MSAU activities at district level. The DEC has its District Patron.

The Division Executive Council (DEC)

The DEC is composed of all MSA chairmen together with an elected executive. The DEC has a Sub- Constitution derived out of the DEC constitution and it details MSAU activities at division level. The DEC has its Division Patron.

The School/Institutional Muslim Students Association (MSA)

The MSA is the most grass root body at school/ Institutional/University level. It is composed of an elected executive. It has a Sub- Constitution derived out of the DEC constitution detailing MSAU work at school level and has a school Patron.
