Women Affairs League

WAL (Women Affairs League) is a sisters wing for the Muslim Studentâ€™s Association of Uganda (MSAU) dedicated to addressing various aspects of women's affairs. WAL operates at University and Tertiary Institutes level. Its mission is to mobilize and sensitize female students to lead respectable lives, enhance self-esteem, and contribute to programs promoting moral uprightness, using Islamic principles as guidance.

WAL aims to bring together female students for development, act as a platform for building self-esteem and confidence, voice female students' issues, promote education and health, counsel on family matters, and encourage leadership among other objectives. Through a range of activities and initiatives, WAL seeks to empower female students with competencies such as good communication, health management, and entrepreneurship, while instilling values like respect, chastity, and perseverance.
