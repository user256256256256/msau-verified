Ramadhan Seminars

The Muslim Students Association of Uganda adopted the concept of organising Annual Ramadhan Seminars in 2016 at various schools and mosques all over the country for Students, Muslim Women and general Muslims from 9:00 am to 4:00 pm. The overall objective of these seminars is to Increase the love for Islam and appreciate the morals of Islam. The seminars are further organized to Underscore the need for Muslim Education, Elucidate the need for good child upbringing and to promote good moral conduct in society. The program of the MSAU Ramadhan Seminars started in 2015 after the realizing the gap in knowledge among the Muslims who were living their own lives and practicing Islam their own ways. The gap of no seminars organized also contributed to the seminars.

How we Mobilize for the Seminars

Planning

  • Prior planning is needed for the success of these seminars. The following need to be done.
  • Pre-seminar meetings with the DEC, REC, NEC, Supreme Council, Imams, District Kadhs, Coordinators and security personnel where need be.
  • Training of the presenters and the imams on the key messages to be delivered.
  • Preparation of the materials and distribution in all the necessary forms available (hard copies and soft copies).
  • Resource mobilization and management (Transport for the facilitators, iftar for the facilitators, coordination package for the facilitators, print outs, registration sheets and any other requirement for the seminars).

Management of the seminars.

The seminars should be properly managed and emphasis should be put on time, presenters and other resources. The seminars should be very interesting and the messages presented should be those already prepared.

Reporting of the seminars

Each seminar conducted should be reported and the reporting should clearly indicate type of seminar, date, venue, aggregated number of participants, presenter and any challenge faced or something unique seen in the seminar using the report template. Photos should also be taken but in a technical way.

Evaluation of the seminars

The team of the organizers are to organize various evaluation meetings every Monday to study the progress of the seminars. The final evaluation meeting shall be organized at the District Level and the MSA level to share experiences and plan for the next Annual Ramadhan Seminars.
