Master Training Course (MTC)

The work camp is the second (middle) training course for MSAU leaders also called Master Training Course (MTC) that is organised for leaders who attended and passed the Basic Training Course (BTC) and need to advance their skills in Community Service. Its aim is to ensure that leaders master and conceptualize the in-depth skills of leadership and management and can apply them in Community Development.

The training program targets middle level managers who have shown much progress in leadership and management. After the MTC leaders can progress to undertake the training for trainers (TOT) and become capable of facilitating at a BTC and qualifies to be champions of community transformation as change agents.

During the BTC, the various concepts are introduced, then during the MTC, the same concepts are handled but with a much deeper analysis and conceptualisation of each concept or aspect.
