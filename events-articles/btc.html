Basic Training Course (BTC)

The (BTC) Basic Training Course is a leadership Training offered to each set of Muslim Students Leaders to equip them with basic skills desired in effective leadership and management of any public office. The course always run for FIVE days and is attended by all leaders as a starting point. Attending two such courses makes one well-grounded in the basic management issues.

This basic training course is aimed at preparing the student leaders to appreciate their role as Muslim Leaders, the key activities that need to be done and how to execute them effectively to bring about the desired results.

Through learning proper planning and execution of office activities, which are solely voluntary and yet so involving, this training is an important step in having leaders who can execute their duties with true knowledge and clear focus.

Therefore, the (BTC) is a basic leadership training course that imparts in the student leaders the key skills and experiences deemed vital in execution of official duties to meet the objectives of Dawah now and in the future.
